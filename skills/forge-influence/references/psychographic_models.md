# Psychographic models

Design guidance for audience segments in influence simulation.

## Purpose

Segment the graph or audience by psychological and behavioral traits so propagation can weight edges by susceptibility and echo-chamber effects.

## Dimensions (example)

- Openness to new narratives vs. entrenched beliefs
- Trust in institutions vs. alternative sources
- Engagement level (lurker vs. amplifier)
- Community allegiance (in-group vs. cross-group)

## Usage

Forge scripts may take segment labels on nodes or a separate profile config; narrative_node and simulate_network use these to adjust propagation probability. No implementation in this phase—document only.
